About NovaDefender

NovaDefender is a passion project developed by a single developer who loves creating security solutions. Our AntiCheat for Minecraft Bedrock is completely free and open to the community, while our premium Anti-malware solution helps fund continued development.

The project started when I noticed the increasing number of security threats online. I decided to use my programming skills to create solutions that would be accessible to everyone, with a free option for gamers and an affordable premium option for complete protection.

NovaDefender is constantly evolving, with regular updates to combat new threats. Your feedback, support, and donations are what keep this project alive and improving!
